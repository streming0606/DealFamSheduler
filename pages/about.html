Our Story

Thrift Maal was born out of a simple frustration: spending hours searching for genuine deals online only to find expired offers or inflated "discounts." Our founder, tired of this experience, decided to create a platform that would do the hard work for shoppers.

What started as a personal project quickly grew into a comprehensive deals platform serving thousands of smart shoppers across India. Today, Thrift Maal stands as a testament to the power of technology in making quality products accessible to everyone.

"We don't just find deals - we find the deals that matter to real people living real lives." - Thrift Maal Team
