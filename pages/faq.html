Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Thrift Maal

How do you find these amazing deals?

We use advanced automated systems that scan thousands of products across major e-commerce platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and others every hour. Our algorithms identify genuine discounts by comparing current prices with historical data and filtering out fake or inflated deals.

Are all the deals authentic and working?

Yes! Every deal on our platform goes through a verification process. We check for actual discounts, product availability, and working links. However, deals can expire or sell out quickly, so we recommend acting fast on offers that interest you.

How often are deals updated?

Our system updates deals every hour, 24/7. This ensures you always see fresh offers and expired deals are quickly removed. We also have manual curation to highlight the best deals of the day.

Why do some deals show "expired" or become unavailable?

Deals can expire due to several reasons: limited-time offers ending, products going out of stock, or merchants changing prices. We try to remove expired deals quickly, but some may slip through due to the dynamic nature of online pricing.

Do I need to pay anything to use Thrift Maal?

No, Thrift Maal is completely free to use! You don't need to pay any subscription fees or membership charges. We may earn commission from purchases made through our links. This doesn't affect the price you pay.

How do affiliate links work? Do they cost me extra?

Affiliate links don't cost you any extra money. When you click our links and make a purchase, we receive a small commission from the retailer. The price you pay remains exactly the same as if you visited the site directly. This is how we keep our service free for users.

Can I return or exchange products I buy through your links?

Yes, all return and exchange policies of the original retailer apply to your purchases. Since we don't sell products directly, you'll need to follow the return procedures of the specific website where you made your purchase (like Amazon, Flipkart, etc.).

Do I need to create an account to browse deals?

No account is required to browse and access deals. However, creating an account allows you to save favorite deals, set up deal alerts, and get personalized recommendations based on your interests.

How can I get notified about new deals in my favorite categories?

You can subscribe to our newsletter or join our Telegram channel for instant deal notifications. We also offer category-specific alerts where you can choose to receive notifications only for electronics, fashion, or other categories you're interested in.

The website is loading slowly. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Clear your browser cache and cookies, 2) Disable browser extensions temporarily, 3) Try a different browser or device, 4) Check your internet connection. If the problem persists, please contact our support team.

Some deal links are not working. What's wrong?

This can happen if: 1) The deal has expired, 2) The product is out of stock, 3) The retailer has changed the URL, or 4) There's a temporary technical issue. Please try refreshing the page or report the broken link to us so we can fix it.

What information do you collect about me?

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service, such as browsing preferences, deal interactions, and contact information if you subscribe to newsletters. We never collect sensitive personal data like payment information. For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.

Do you share my data with third parties?

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform, and only when necessary. All data sharing is covered in our Privacy Policy and done with appropriate safeguards.

How can I suggest a deal or report a problem?

We love hearing from our users! You can contact us through the Contact page, send an email to support@thriftzone.com, or reach out through our social media channels. We read every message and try to respond within 24 hours.

Can I use Thrift Maal on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our website is fully optimized for mobile devices and tablets. You get the same great experience on any device. We're also working on a dedicated mobile app for even better mobile shopping experience.
